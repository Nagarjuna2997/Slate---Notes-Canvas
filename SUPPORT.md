# Support

**Slate - Notes & Canvas**

---

## About the App

Slate is a minimal notes and canvas app for iOS. Write, sketch, and organize your ideas in one beautiful, distraction-free space. Combine rich text editing with an infinite canvas — powered by smart blocks, Apple Pencil support, and flexible export options.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Slate free to use?

Slate is available on the App Store. Check the App Store listing for current pricing and any available free features.

### Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, your data may also sync across your Apple devices through Apple's iCloud service. The developer never has access to your data.

### Does Slate collect any personal information?

No. Slate does not collect, track, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no ads, and no third-party trackers. See the full [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).

### How do I use smart blocks?

Type `/` in any note to open the block picker. From there you can insert text, headings, lists, tables, charts, math equations (LaTeX), images, audio recordings, files, or a canvas.

### What canvas templates are available?

Slate offers four canvas templates:
- **Blank** — an empty infinite canvas
- **Grid** — square grid pattern
- **Dotted** — dot grid pattern
- **Lined** — horizontal lines

### Does Slate support Apple Pencil?

Yes. Slate has full Apple Pencil support on the infinite canvas. You can draw, sketch, and write naturally with pressure sensitivity.

### What export formats are supported?

You can export your notes as:
- Plain Text (.txt)
- Markdown (.md)
- PDF (.pdf)
- Word (.rtf)

### How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Slate supports both light and dark mode, including a pure black OLED theme. You can configure this in the app's settings. Slate also follows your system appearance setting.

## Report a Bug

Found something that isn't working correctly? Please report it so it can be fixed.

**How to report:**

1. Go to [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Nagarjuna2997/Slate---Notes-Canvas/issues)
2. Click **New Issue**
3. Include the following details:
   - **Device model** (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Air)
   - **iOS version** (e.g., iOS 17.4)
   - **App version** (found in Settings)
   - **Steps to reproduce** the issue
   - **What you expected** to happen
   - **What actually happened**
   - Screenshots or screen recordings if possible

Alternatively, email the details to [nagarjunareddy2997@icloud.com](mailto:nagarjunareddy2997@icloud.com).
