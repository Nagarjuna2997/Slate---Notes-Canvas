# Privacy Policy

**Slate - Notes & Canvas**
*Last updated: February 9, 2026*

---

## Overview

Slate ("the App") is developed by Nagarjuna. Your privacy is important. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

**The short version: Slate does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.**

---

## Data Collection

Slate does **not** collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- **No usage analytics** or telemetry is gathered
- **No crash reports** are sent to the developer
- **No advertising identifiers** are used
- **No cookies or tracking technologies** are employed

## Data Storage

All data you create in Slate — including notes, notebooks, canvases, and preferences — is stored **locally on your device**. The developer has no access to your content.

## iCloud Sync

If you use iCloud with Slate, your data may sync across your Apple devices through Apple's iCloud service. This sync is handled entirely by Apple and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). The developer does not have access to your iCloud data.

## Third-Party Services

Slate does **not** integrate with any third-party services. There are:

- No third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Firebase, etc.)
- No third-party advertising frameworks
- No social media SDKs
- No third-party data processors

## Children's Privacy

Slate does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Sharing

Since Slate does not collect any data, there is no data to share, sell, or transfer to any third party.

## Data Security

Your data remains on your device under the security protections provided by iOS, including device encryption and your passcode or biometric authentication.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
